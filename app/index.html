XB Entertainment was founded in 2019 following the realization of the scope of software piracy and the inability of software developers to properly secure the use of their software.

XB Entertainment is an advanced licensing software solution with many features, including integration with 3rd party eCommerce providers, multiple licensing models, flexible and custom integration, real time, random activation code generation, and much more.
